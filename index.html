Van Gogh: Life and Art

1853

Vincent van Gogh was born in Zundert on 30 March.

1860

At the age of eleven, Vincent transferred from the village school in Zundert to a boarding school in Zevenbergen.

1867-1868

He leaves school for unknown reasons and never comes back.

1869-1872

Vincent's uncle got him a job at the international gallery Goupil e Cie. In 1872 Vincent was transferred to Goupil’s London branch.

1873-1875

In London, Vincent visited famous institutions like the British Museum and the National Gallery. In 1875, Vicent was transferred to Paris, a time when he became very religious.

1876

Vicent was not attracted to his work and in 1876 the company demissed him. Vincent returned to England, where he worked as an unpaid assistant teacher at a boys’ boarding school in Ramsgate.
